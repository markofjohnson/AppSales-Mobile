AppSales is a tool to download your App Store sales reports from iTunes Connect directly on your iPhone.

AppSales was created by Ole Zorn with help from the open source community. You can always find the latest version at http://github.com/omz/AppSales-Mobile.

You can also follow me on Twitter, and if you like, check out my commercial iPhone apps at omz-software.com.

AppSales downloads the reports from http://itunesconnect.apple.com. If this website changes significantly, AppSales might not work properly. In this case, I'll try to provide an update ASAP. Please feel free to notify me of any bugs or strange behaviour you notice at http://github.com/omz/AppSales-Mobile.

AppSales downloads currency exchange rates from the web to display your revenue in local currency. Please note that the displayed (current) exchange rates might not reflect your actual payout on the day your money is transferred. It is meant as a rough guideline.

The reports you'll see in AppSales are to be considered as trend reports. Please do NOT regard them as a substitute to your monthly royalty reports that you can download at http://itunesconnect.apple.com. I do NOT guarantee that either the reports or the displayed currency rates are accurate. Please download backup copies of the original report files on your computer.
